# TODO-013: Re-issue Tickets

## Overview
Allow organizers to re-send tickets to attendees who have lost them.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] "Re-issue Tickets" button on registration
- [ ] Option to send to different email
- [ ] Generate new ticket PDFs
- [ ] Include QR codes
- [ ] Track re-issue history
- [ ] Invalidate old tickets (optional)
- [ ] Bulk re-issue for multiple attendees

## Re-issue Features
1. **Individual Re-issue**
   - Original email or alternate
   - Add note about re-issue
   - Track who requested
   
2. **Bulk Re-issue**
   - Multiple attendees at once
   - After email change
   - For technical issues

3. **Security Options**
   - Invalidate previous QR codes
   - Note in check-in system
   - Audit trail

## Technical Requirements
- Regenerate ticket PDFs
- New or same QR codes
- Email delivery
- Track all re-issues
- Update ticket status

## Why This Next
Common support request, especially close to events.

## Definition of Done
- Tickets re-sent successfully
- PDFs generated correctly
- QR codes work
- History tracked
- Email delivery confirmed
