## Onboarding Steps
1. **Welcome & Benefits**
   - Explain why connect bank
   - Show fee structure (2.5%)
   - Security assurances
   
2. **Lodge Information**
   - Lodge name and number
   - ABN (if applicable)
   - Meeting address
   - Grand Lodge jurisdiction
   
3. **Bank Account**
   - Account holder name
   - BSB and account number
   - Confirm lodge account
   
4. **Officers**
   - Master details
   - Secretary details  
   - Treasurer details
   
5. **Documents**
   - Upload lodge charter
   - Grand Lodge certificate
   - Bank statement
   
6. **Terms & Submit**
   - Accept terms
   - Submit for verification

## Why This First in V3
Cannot receive payments without connected account.

## Definition of Done
- Smooth step-by-step flow
- Progress saves automatically
- Clear error messages
- Documents upload successfully
- Mobile friendly
